<br>

We present in this paper the latest news about the adaptive optics facility bench LOOPS at
LAM. A review of the scheme of the bench is first given, going through the optical path module after module.
The first results obtained with a spatial light modulator, used to produce high-definition reprogrammable focal phase mask, are then presented.
